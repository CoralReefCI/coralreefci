Docs

Q: How do I install Heupr on my repo?

A: It's a breeze! Just click "SIGN UP NOW!" on this website's landing page which will direct you to the GitHub installation and user authentication pages.

Q: Why do I need to sign in to GitHub?

A: It allows us to present you with the relevent options for repos and labels to choose in your settings as well as provide a layer of security.

Q: What are "default" and "classification" labels?

A: Great question! "Default" labels are ones you want applied to every incoming issue while "classification" are the ones you want Heupr to use to identify bug/improvement/feature issues.

Q: Where do I see Heupr's activity?

A: It all takes place in GitHub's webpages. You'll see contributor assignments and label selections on their respective fiels on incoming issues.

Q: Can I change Heupr's choices?

A: You sure can! Make whatever changes you need directly through GitHub like normal.

Q: Why I don't see assignments and/or labels on a new issue?

A: Heupr may have hit a GitHub rate limit - if you don't see an update by then, try refreshing the issue page. If that doesn't work, give us a shout and we'll look right into it!

Q: What data do you use for training?

A: Heupr uses prior issue assignments and issue text fields to ascertain the best contributors to handle the issue and which labels best define the issue. Currently, we only use public data.

Q: Do you support private and enterprise repos?

A: Not yet, but we plan on offering that soon!

Q: Where can I get in touch with you?

A: Hit us up at heuprHQ@gmail.com for general inquiries and feedback on our app. Also, if you're interested in demo user assignments or label selections for your repo, let us know!

Q: Where does the name "Heupr" come from?

A: It's a portmanteau of the words "heuristic" and "projects" (together pronounced "HUE-per") and is a reminder of our goal to help people make smart projects!

Q: Where can I follow Heupr's progress?

Follow us at Twitter for general project updates. Also, feel free to sign up at our Product Hunt Upcoming page for feature launches and beta announcements.
